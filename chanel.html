Founder

Gabrielle Bonheur Chanel (August 19, 1883 to January 10, 1971)

First known product

LGabrielle "Coco" Chanel's first product was hats, which she began selling in
1910 at her boutique Chanel Modes in Paris

Headquarters

In 2018, Chanel announced relocation of its global headquarters to London.

Expensive product

Chanel's most expensive product is the Diamond Forever bag, which sold for $261,000 in 2023.
This bag is made of alligator leather and features white gold hardware, including a clasp with 334 diamonds

Brand ambassador

Lupita Nyong'o

Best selling perfume

Chanel No. 5 is the first perfume launched by French couturier Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel in 1921.
The scent formula for the fragrance was compounded by French-Russian chemist and perfumer Ernest Beaux.
